Core Values

Our History.

WINSTAR INT'L ACADEMY is a dynamic educational institution incorporated on 3rd of February, 2015 by seasoned educators with special interest in educational technology, curriculum studies, teachers education and early childhood learning with the use of modern facilities.

The academy located at a serene environment operates a full day school system with our qualified workforce in place, Winstar International Academy uses the innovative and participatory methodology through Montessori and play way system.

Ours is to understand the need and requirements of each child and match it with more appropriate instructional strategy and materials to ensure effective and efficient learning through well articulate curriculum and foreign guideline.

Some of Our Schools

All of the schools in the Trust share a common purpose: to offer outstanding, varied and inspirational education to children.

The Nursery is bright and airy, with plenty of green space. Open 36 weeks of the year from 8.00am to 2.15pm, our experienced and qualified staff nurture, encourage and support all the children, ensuring they feel both happy and secure in our care. The children have use of all the school facilities and access to specialist teachers.

Nursery pupils are taught through an exciting, broad and innovative curriculum that stimulates and inspires their curious minds. Class sizes are small and the children grow by exploring knowledge both inside and outside the classroom. With access to all school facilities and specialist teachers we offer a wide breadth of subjects, including English, Maths, Science and PE.

Our Primary School instils values of high academic achievement, without compromising the need to play and let off steam. Pupils are encouraged to take responsibility for their possessions, time-keeping and work. The day lengthens and the focus on academic attainment heightens. The ultimate aim is for pupils to fulfil their own highest academic expectations and gain a place at their chosen Senior School.

We operate a secondary school with the best practices such as flipped learning, multiple intelligence and scientific approach which are our all inclusive approach to learning.
