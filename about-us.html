A Unique Learning Community

Our mission places a high value on the notion of being "Unique."

WIA exists solely to “nurture Unique individuals to aim high, accomplish, and make a significant contribution.” We prepare our students by providing them with 21st Century innovative skills, with a particular emphasis on creativity, critical thinking, communication, collaboration, and fortitude. It is critical that students are equipped with the skills necessary to flourish in an ever-changing environment.

When the future looks so very different from what it is today, being prepared for it requires forward thinking and a robust understanding of current trends in a multitude of different areas. What impact will technology have on schooling? What impact will the future of employment have on education? Just how will we prepare balanced global citizens with compassionate hearts in an increasingly stressful, secular, internationalized, technology-driven and fast changing world

Our goal is to provide learning that will prep each student for his or her coming years.
